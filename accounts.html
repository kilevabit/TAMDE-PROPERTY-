Company Accounts & Responsibilities

Property Manager

  • Manage tenants and leases
  • Supervise property maintenance
  • Ensure rent collection
  • Prepare management reports

Accountant

  • Record rent payments
  • Track expenses
  • Prepare financial statements

Maintenance Officer

  • Handle repair requests
  • Inspect properties regularly
  • Ensure safety compliance

Tenant

  • Pay rent on time
  • Report maintenance issues
  • Follow lease agreement
